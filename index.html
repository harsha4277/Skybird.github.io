Disease Treatment Guide

White Gut Disease
Symptoms:
  • White coloration in gut area
  • Reduced feeding activity
  • Slow growth rates
  • Increased mortality
Recommended Products:
Healthy Gut Pro 360
Vibriosis
Symptoms:
  • Shell lesions and necrosis
  • Muscle opacity
  • Reduced activity
  • High mortality rates
Recommended Products:
Healthy Gut Pro 360 Doctor Cure
Ammonia Toxicity
Symptoms:
  • Gasping at water surface
  • Gill damage and inflammation
  • Lethargy and stress
  • Poor feed conversion
Recommended Products:
Ammo Cure Gaso Curb Zero Sludge
Bacterial Infections
Symptoms:
  • Red spots on body
  • Fin rot and tail rot
  • Ulcers and lesions
  • Behavioral changes
Recommended Products:
Doctor Cure Pro 360
Poor Digestion
Symptoms:
  • Poor feed conversion ratio
  • Slow growth
  • Weak immune system
  • Digestive disorders
Recommended Products:
Nutrizyme Healthy Gut
Pond Bottom Pollution
Symptoms:
  • Foul smell from pond
  • Black pond bottom
  • High H2S levels
  • Poor water quality
Recommended Products:
Suraksha Zero Sludge Gaso Curb
